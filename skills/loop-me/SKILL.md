# Loop Me

Find a repeated outcome with a clear trigger, inputs, owner, failure boundary,
and observable result. Grill the workflow until its decisions, authority, and
exceptions are explicit. Reject one-off chores and workflows that cannot be
verified safely.

When the loop is settled, route it through `/to-spec` and `/to-tickets`; use
`/wayfinder` if key decisions remain foggy. Do not create hidden schedules or
automation as part of discovery.
