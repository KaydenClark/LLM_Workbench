# Wayfinder

Wayfinding finds the route before delivery. Name a destination, read the
nearest controls, and write only the currently knowable decision frontier in
the owning stable `SPEC.md`. Keep unresolved but still-vague work in its
decision log; promote it to one-context tickets only when the question is
sharp.

For each session, choose one unblocked decision, use `/grilling`,
`/research`, `/prototype`, or `/domain-modeling` as its shape requires, then
record the conclusion and evidence in that spec. Render and doctor after a
metadata change. Stop when the path is clear and hand execution to
`/to-tickets` or `/implement`; do not turn route-finding into unapproved
delivery.
