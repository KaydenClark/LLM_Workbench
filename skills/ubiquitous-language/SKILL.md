# Ubiquitous Language

Collect terms from the current conversation, controls, and source. Identify
synonyms, overloaded words, and contradictions; use concrete examples to
settle one meaning at a time. Propose precise preferred terms and surface any
choice that changes authority or scope.

After agreement, update the nearest owning `LEXICON.md` with concise
definitions and links to any decision that explains why. Keep requirements,
work state, and implementation detail in their existing homes.
